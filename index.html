About us

Generating critical neurological knowledge

More than 80% of the world's population owns a smartphone. With Mon4t, 80% of the world has access to a neurological assessment device. Get a comprehensive clinical view of motor, cognitive, and affective status quickly and easily with a simple, intuitive app—remotely or in person. Use novel data for early diagnosis of diseases such as Parkinson's, Huntington's, Alzheimer's and others. FDA-approved and HIPAA-compliant, Mon4t improves medical decision-making with detailed short- and long-term information about clinical neurological symptoms.
